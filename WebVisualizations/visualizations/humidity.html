Latitude vs. Humidity

Based on the current available data there does not appear to be a relationship between latitude and humidity. Cities between -40 and 0 latitude as well as cities between 20 and 60 appear to have lower humidity levels, so there may be a relationship to discover, but this would require more investigation.
